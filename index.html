ABOUT rushabh

Rushabh is a self taught pianist who has allowed his different musical experiences to create a unique fusion of Western and Eastern sounds. After beginning his journey into Hindustani classical music, his desire to express this to a global audience through the keys of a piano has only ever grown. His improvisational style gives his fingers the physical creativity born from the soul of a raga.
